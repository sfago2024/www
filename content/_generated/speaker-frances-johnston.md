From there, she went to USC to study with Ladd Thomas. Since then, she has served in a variety of churches and taught high school choral music and handbells. She currently serves part-time at All Saints Anglican Cathedral in Long Beach, CA.
Fran has been active in the Pasadena (now merged with Los Angeles), Orange County and Long Beach chapters of the A.G.O., having served as Dean of both Pasadena and Orange County.
